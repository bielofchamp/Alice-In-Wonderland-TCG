Welcome to the Official Rules Manual section for our Alice in Wonderland card game. Here, you will find all detailed guidelines for playing, from initial setup to advanced dueling rules.
Developed by Eduardo and Gabi, this manual is essential to ensuring that all players have a fair and exciting experience.

tutorial

Who to play
TCG

Cards must be played with the information apparent on them.

The game must be played with 2 groups of 3 players.

Card deck: Must be placed on the right side of the battlefield. This is where the invocations will take place.

Normal Summon: Must be used by all players every turn. Costs 20 happiness.

Special Summon: Can be used when eliminating an enemy player. It costs 10 happiness and can be used whenever the player wishes.

Graveyard: Where cards go when destroyed. Must be used on the left side of the battlefield.

Battlefield: this is where the card duel should take place, in the center between the 6 players.



rules

Rules

Rule 1: Each player must start with 1 card. Rule 2: Every turn, all players must summon at least 1 card.

Rule 3: To summon a card costs 20 happiness.

Rule 4: All players start with 100 happiness and can only recover it by eliminating another player.

Rule 5: When you eliminate a player you receive 50 happiness and you can only recover it by eliminating another player.

Rule 6: When you lose a card you must place it in the cemetery.

Rule 7: The group will only be eliminated if all players in the group have no more cards.



shifts

shifts

Attack: During their turn, the player can attack any enemy player they wish.

Defense: When attacked, the player must defend himself with one of the cards in his hand, if the defense is good.

Trap: When using a trap card it will remain on the battlefield and will be activated when one of the allied players is attacked.
