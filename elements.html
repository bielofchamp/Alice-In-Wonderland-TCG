Our vision

We believe games are more than entertainment – they are an art form, a way of telling stories and an opportunity to explore new worlds. With Alice in Yu-Gi-Oh Land, our goal is to transport players to a universe where imagination has no limits and every decision can change the course of the adventure. The game n Yu-Gi-Oh Land combines the strategy and card collection elements of Yu-Gi-Oh! with an engaging narrative inspired by Lewis Carroll's classic. Players embark on a journey through a fantastical world, facing intriguing challenges and opponents while exploring stunning landscapes and discovering hidden secrets. Each card and character has been carefully created to enrich the gaming experience, ensuring hours of fun and discovery.

Our compromise

We are committed to providing an exceptional gaming experience. We value player feedback and are always looking for ways to improve and expand Alice in Yu-Gi-Oh Land. Our goal is to create a game that is accessible and enjoyable for everyone, offering a perfect balance between challenge and fun. Join us on this incredible adventure and discover a world where imagination reigns and each letter could be the key to a new mystery!

Definition

Founders

Founders are the visionaries and pioneers who conceive, establish and develop an organization, project or enterprise since its inception. They are responsible for defining the initial mission, values, and goals, as well as leading the team and making strategic decisions that guide the initiative's continued growth and success.

Programmers

Programmers are professionals specialized in creating, developing and maintaining software (lol). They write code in various programming languages, transforming requirements and specifications into functional applications. Responsible for solving technical problems, optimizing performance and implementing new features, programmers play a crucial role in technological innovation and building digital solutions.

Designers

Designers are creative professionals specialized in conceiving and creating visuals and aesthetic experiences. Working in diverse areas such as graphics, product, fashion, user interface (UI) and user experience (UX), they use artistic and technical skills to create functional and attractive designs. Its objective is to combine form and function, improving the usability, communication and visual appeal of products and services.

Creator of rules

A creator of rules is an individual responsible for establishing the guidelines, principles, and frameworks that govern a system or activity. This person defines the parameters within which participants must operate, ensuring consistency, fairness, and clarity. In various contexts, from games to organizations, the creator of rules plays a crucial role in shaping the structure and dynamics, promoting order and facilitating smooth functioning.
